Red Velvet Chocolate Chip Cookies

I have loved Red Velvet Cookies since I was a child. I was shocked when I found out that red velvet is just another kind of chocolate. I didn't know what else red velvet would be, but nevertheless I was still shocked.

The bold dark red of these cookies is enough to make your mouth water. When you finally sink your teeth into these delectable cookies, you fell the full force of the FLAVOR!

  • All-purpose flour
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk
  1. Whisk Flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Beat butter with an electric mixer until fluffy, about 2 minutes; beat in brown sugar and white sugar until smooth, about 1 minute. Beat egg, milk, and vanilla extract into butter mixture; beat in food coloring until uniformly colored.
  3. Stir flour mixture into butter mixture. Stir in chocolate chips
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  5. Roll dough into balls
  6. Bake dough in oven
